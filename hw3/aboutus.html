About us

One fateful afternoon, before the weekly movie night, a Depop order was delivered to our house. My househouse picked it up, and it fit her perfectly! Honoring the spirit of the movie Sisterhood of the Traveling Pants we all decided to try it on. And lo and behold, it fit all of us!

The wonder of these magical pants really set us on a journey to strive and create pants that fit people of all shapes and sizes. Maybe one pair won't fit you, but you are certain to find another pair that does on our online store!

The names of the four college students who started this company does not matter, for we shall fade with history. However, you can contact us here, and stick around. Maybe even join the tailorhood!
